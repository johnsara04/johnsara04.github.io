Firefly

Jan 29, 2017 by John

Stage One

The model from PSC goes together really well with little flash

Ive built with hatch open for a commander but will use a battlefront British commander as I don’t like the psc ones on the purely subjective reason that I don’t. I’ve also undercoated black as I (again subjectively) think it gives a bit of heft to the subsequent painting.

Next up is a coat of Olive Drab shadow. Its Mig Ammo range this time but I have used AK interactive in the past. I go all over for speed. I could just apply in the areas where it requires shadow but that's slower…

Once the coat is dry it’s a case of starting with modulation painting going through the Ammo paints from base to shine

Once complete it is ready for a filter.
